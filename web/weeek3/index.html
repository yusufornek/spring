Patrik Meyer, an expert in the field, will explore the implications of advanced Artificial Intelligence in our daily lives and the workplace. As AI continues to evolve, it raises fundamental questions about the role of humans in a world where machines can perform a myriad of tasks. Mr. Meyer will delve into the challenges and opportunities that arise when AI reaches a point where it can fulfill nearly all functions traditionally carried out by humans. This seminar is a must-attend for professionals, researchers, and students interested in the intersection of technology, business, and society. Gain valuable insights into the future of work, human-AI collaboration, and the profound impact of AI on various industries. Don't miss the opportunity to engage in an enlightening discussion and broaden your understanding of the evolving landscape shaped by Artificial Intelligence. We look forward to your participation in this MIS Talks seminar.
